BIO: ewoud is a Belgian composer, multimedia artist and musician working in the field of instrumental improvisation, performance, 3d audio and electronics

his practice spans across developing instruments for transmedial trance exploration, composing for and performing with ensembles and glittery audiovisual installations. He graduated as a Nordic jazz master in Helsinki, Stockholm and Aarhus and he is the founder of the immersive Lovely Spaces festival, based in Aarhus, Denmark.
