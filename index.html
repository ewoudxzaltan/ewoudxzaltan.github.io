ewoud Van Eetvelde is a guitar player, composer, multidisciplinary improviser, educator and video/electronic artist from Belgium, currently based in Aarhus, Denmark. His works and projects include multidisciplinary collaborations in dance, theater and music. Musical formations in rock, jazz and electronics - as a bandleader or side-man. Solo-projects and collaborations in generative electronic processing of audio and video. His work revolves around experimentalism, unbound imagination, inviting synesthesia and shifting perspectives throughout a multidimensional framework. Inviting complexity, while yet framing it very precisely. “(…) but simple elements can combine in complex ways, as atoms become molecules and molecules become life”. *
